Who we are?

Museum & Interconnect Arequipa is a company dedicated to offering program visits to the multiple Museums in Arequipa City.

Arequipa

Arequipa, known as the "White City" for its stunning volcanic sillar architecture, boasts a rich cultural scene with museums that bring its history, art, and traditions to life. The Museo Santuarios Andinos, The Museo de Arte Virreinal de Santa Teresa, the Museo del Monasterio de Santa Catalina, The Museo de Arte Contemporáneo Arequipa the Museo Arqueológico UNSA are some of the most important museums that create a vivid journey through Arequipa’s past and present, making the city a cultural hub in southern Peru.

Type of Visits

Classic Historical Tour

  • A guided visit covering the museum’s most important collections
  • offering historical context and key facts
  • perfect for first-time visitors.

In-Depth Specialist Tour

  • Led by an expert (historian, archaeologist, or art curator)
  • focusing on specific artifacts, periods, or artistic styles for visitors seeking a deeper understanding.

Family & Kids Tour

  • Interactive tour adapted for children
  • Using games, storytelling, and hands-on activities to make learning fun for the whole family.

Nighttime Cultural Tour

  • Evening visit with atmospheric lighting, live music
  • Special performances that bring the museum’s stories to life
